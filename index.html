ILFA Students' Union

The ILFA Students' Union of the 10th Batch at SFADC Odamala is a student representative body established to promote unity, leadership, and the overall development of students. It serves as a platform where students can actively participate in academic, cultural, social, and extracurricular activities while fostering cooperation and mutual respect among classmates.
